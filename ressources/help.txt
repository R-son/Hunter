

USAGE
    ./my_hunter

LAUNCHES A GAME OF MY HUNTER.

ON TITLE SCREEN PRESS THE SPACEBAR TO START THE GAME AND ESCAPE TO LEAVE THE GAME.
IN GAME, CLICK ON THE GHOST TO SHOOT IT. IF YOU MISS 3 GHOSTS, YOU'LL REACH GAME OVER.
ON THE GAME OVER SCREEN IF YOU PRESS SPACEBAR, YOU'LL START A NEW GAME, PRESS ESCAPE TO QUIT AND P TO RETURN TO THE TITLE SCREEN.
